About Me

My name is Samantha Mack and I am a front-end web developer with an occupational therapy background geared towards building an inclusive web experience for all users. Recently completed and earned a certificate in full stack development from the University of Irvine Coding Bootcamp, with newly developed skills in JavaScript, CSS, React.js, and responsive web-design. I’m flexible and motivated in developing web and mobile apps that will provide users an impactful and memorable experience. The purpose in each project I’m involved in is aimed at ensuring all users, regardless of their abilities, experience web-apps in the most efficient manner. I’m eager to apply the skills and knowledge gained during boot-camp and enhance my ever expanding knowledge in coding in building more applicable and memorable web apps. In my spare time my hobbies include binge watching tv shows, swimming and gardening. I am also a big fan of watching sports, especially figure skating, baseball- Go Yankees!, and the Olympics!
